🔒
Financial records cannot be deleted from this system. Transactions, receipts, the verification log, and the audit log are permanent by design (noDeleteAllowed / transactionImmutable). No control in this app — including the option below — can erase them. This is intentional and cannot be overridden from the UI.
Starting a Fresh Financial Period?
Don't delete old data — export a full backup of the database first, then move that file to archival storage (e.g. cold storage, a separate encrypted drive). The ledger here stays complete and queryable; archiving just relocates your copy of closed-period data out of daily view.
Reset App Configuration & Login
Clears business profile, PIN/password, and app settings only — for "I'm locked out / misconfigured the setup" cases. Does not touch transactions, receipts, verification log, or audit log. You'll be returned to onboarding to set up credentials again.
